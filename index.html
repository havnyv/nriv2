Core Features Offered by the NRI Institute

  • 1

    Campus

    Our campus is moderate with spacious class rooms, laboratories, digital library, Seminar hall, HR room for placement interviews, and student common rooms.

  • 2

    Placement

    All our passed out students are well settled with good placements and many are in senior positions due to their gained experience after their completion of studies in our institution.

  • 3

    Hostels

    Hostels rooms are spacious enough to accommodate 3-4 students per room. Sufficient separate row bath rooms and fresh up rooms are provided in each floor.

  • 4

    Canteen

    As we have students from different states of India and abroad menu is on rotation and is always prepared in consultation with the students.

  • 5

    Recreation and sports

    Teacher- student fun sports, Fresher's day and graduation functions are our regular annual activities. Efforts are on to provide indoor Gym for physical health.

  • 6

    Library

    Spacious and well stocked with curriculum books and general reading books to improve students self development to gain more moral values one would like to have for themself.

About NRI Institute

Home→About

Her prime motto is to provide a matchless educational assistance to every student child of all respective classes. She devoted her precious time, efforts, and life to interpret her ambition into a reality. The institution strives to fulfill its mission to provide educational opportunities to all aspiring youth to excel in life by developing academic excellence, fostering values, creating civic responsibility and building global competencies in a dynamic environment.

The college curriculum comprises of Pre- University Courses as well as Degree Courses. Our major course streams on offer are in the science, commerce and Management. Further breaking down the genres, we offer specializations in Commerce, Computer Science, Management and Business.

The college also offers a host of value Added Courses for students to enhance their skills and develop special talents. The College is supported by highly committed and qualified faculty, who have extensive industry, research and consulting experience. The faculty comprises of well trained academicians who strive to help the students to achieve their goals. The college offers economic assistance to meritorious students by providing scholarships. The college comprises of separate boys and girls hostels with 24/7 security.
